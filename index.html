Controls ×

You can use the following controls:

  • W - Move forward
  • S - Move backward
  • A - Move left
  • D - Move right
  • Oxygen Meter - You get 100 units of oxygen
  • If you need help - Click The Cat
  • Esc - Restart/Leave game
  • Mouse - Look around and click
